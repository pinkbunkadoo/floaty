What's all this?
Floaty is an app for turning your images into transparent desktop windows. As with regular windows, an image window can be resized and positioned. The scale, opacity and placement of each image, within its frame, can also be adjusted as needed.

Getting started
  1. To begin, simply drag one or more images onto the drop area
  2. Make desired adjustments to your image windows
  3. Fix your images in place by tapping the eye icon, above, or by pressing slash (/) on the keyboard *
* While in the fixed state, you will be unable to interact with image windows. Tap the Floaty tray icon to restore the main window and unfix all images.
