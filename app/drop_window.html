What's all this?
Floaty is an app for turning images into transparent desktop windows. As with regular windows, an image window can be resized and positioned. The scale, opacity and placement of each image, within its frame, can also be adjusted as needed.

Getting started
  1. To begin, simply drag one or more images onto the drop area
  2. Make desired adjustments to your image windows
  3. Fix the images in place by tapping the eye icon, above, or by pressing slash (/) on the keyboard *
  4. Tap the Floaty tray icon to restore the main window and unfix all images
* While in the fixed state, you cannot interact with image windows
