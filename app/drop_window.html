Floaty takes images and turns them into transparent desktop windows. As with a regular window, the size and position of each image can be adjusted. The opacity, scale and placement of the image, within its frame, can also be altered.

How?
To begin, simply drag one or more images onto the drop area. A separate window will be created for each image. To freeze the images in place click the eye icon or press the '/' key. The main window will disappear and an icon will appear in the system tray area. Click the tray icon to unfreeze the images and reveal the main window.
